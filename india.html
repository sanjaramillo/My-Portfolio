Heart of Yoga Journey

Web Design & Print

About This Project

Developed the look and feel for this yoga retreat event, celebrated every year on the month of February. Inspired by the different locations visited during the retreat as well as some of the traditional Indian textures and colors, I generated a unique style that could be applied in all kinds of media elements, from the event website to the promotional flyer, giving the whole campaign a sense of unity and customer recognition.
